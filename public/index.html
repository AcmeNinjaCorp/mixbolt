Welcome to MixBolt, a DJ tool for your browser. This software is open source, licensed under LGPL v3. You can find the code, license and instructions at github.com/adaline/mixbolt.

To get started, just drag a file from your computer onto either deck and hit play.

This project was developed over a 2-year period by Valentin Arkhipov, you can follow the news on twitter: @mixbolt, I hope this project lets everyone enjoy their music in a new way :)
